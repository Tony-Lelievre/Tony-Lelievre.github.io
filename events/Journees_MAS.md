### Journées thématiques du groupe SMAI - MAS: Metastability and stochastic processes

<!-------------- TITRE -------------------------><br>

<img style="float: right; width: 370px; height: 264px;" src="../../assets/Journees_MAS/metastable_traj.gif">
<img style="width: 348px; height: 258px; float: left;" src="../../assets/Journees_MAS/metastable_potential.gif">

#### September 21st - 23rd 2011, Ecole des Ponts ParisTech


<!-------------- ACCROCHE ------------------------->
<p>The purpose of this workshop is to bring together scientists from the
applied probability community to discuss&nbsp;problems related to the
longtime behaviour of stochastic processes used in various fields of
the stochastic modelling: molecular dynamics, numerical statistical
mechanics, population dynamics, etc. In particular, we would like to
present recent tools which have been developed for the&nbsp;numerical
simulation and the mathematical analysis of metastable stochastic
processes. We also intend to cover related topics: quasi-stationary
distributions, non-equilibrium statistical mechanics, coarse-graining
techniques in molecular dynamics, for example.</p>
