### Rheology of complex fluids: modeling and numerics
#### January 5 - 9th 2009, &Eacute;cole des Ponts - Peking University joint workshop

<center>
<a href="../../assets/Workshop_Complex_Fluid/DSC01889.JPG"><img width="300" alt="photo of participants" src="../../assets/Workshop_Complex_Fluid/DSC01889_low.jpg"></a>
</center>

<!-------------- ACCROCHE ------------------------->
The purpose of this workshop is to bring together applied
mathematicians and experts from&nbsp;mechanics working in the field of
simulation and modeling of complex fluids, with an emphasis on stochastic
modeling. Modeling questions
(<span style="font-style: italic;">e.g.</span> micro-macro <span style="font-style: italic;">versus</span> macro),&nbsp; numerical questions
(discussion of appropriate discretization schemes) and theoretical
questions (<span style="font-style: italic;">e.g.</span> well posedness of various models) are expected to be addressed.<br><br>Link to this webpage: <a href="http://www.enpc.fr/complexfluids">www.enpc.fr/complexfluids/</a><br><br>
